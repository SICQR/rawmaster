Frequently Asked
Questions

How long does processing take? expand_more
BPM/key detection takes approximately 5 seconds. Remastering runs in around 10 seconds. Stem separation takes 5-15 minutes on CPU or 1-3 minutes with a GPU. MIDI extraction completes in roughly 10 seconds.
What are the system requirements? expand_more
Python 3.9+, macOS/Windows/Linux, approximately 1GB disk space. GPU is optional but recommended for faster stem separation. 8GB RAM recommended.
What does reference mastering do? expand_more
Matches EQ, loudness, and dynamics to a professional reference track of your choosing. Runs entirely locally and costs nothing after purchase.
Can I use outputs commercially? expand_more
Yes. Your license, your outputs.
What audio formats are supported? expand_more
MP3, WAV, FLAC, AIFF, OGG, and M4A. Output is 24-bit WAV at 44.1kHz.
What's the refund policy? expand_more
