Privacy Policy

RAWMASTER Browser Extension — Last updated: 14 April 2026

The RAWMASTER browser extension does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data.

What the extension does

RAWMASTER injects a processing button into supported AI music platform pages. When clicked, the extension sends the audio URL to a local Companion application running on your own machine at 127.0.0.1:5432. All audio processing happens locally. Nothing is sent to external servers.

Data the extension accesses

The extension reads audio URLs from the web page you are viewing. This data is sent only to the local Companion daemon on your machine. It is not transmitted to any external server, cloud service, or third party.

Data the extension stores

User preferences (stem count, MIDI toggle, quality setting) are stored locally using Chrome's storage.local API. This data never leaves your browser.

What we do NOT collect

  • Browsing history
  • Account information from any platform
  • Audio content or metadata
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Cookies or tracking data
  • Personal information of any kind

Network requests

The extension makes requests only to 127.0.0.1:5432 (your local RAWMASTER Companion daemon). No other network requests are made.

Third-party platforms

The extension processes your own generated content from supported platforms. Use of the extension must comply with the terms of service of any platform you use it with. We are not affiliated with or endorsed by any third-party platform.
