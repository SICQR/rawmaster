RAWMASTER for Suno (Chrome Extension)

RAWMASTER for Suno does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data.

What this extension does

RAWMASTER for Suno injects a button into suno.com track cards. When clicked, the extension sends the audio URL of the selected track to the RAWMASTER Companion app running locally on your machine at http://127.0.0.1:5432. The Companion processes the audio entirely on your device and returns a ZIP file, which is downloaded directly to your computer.

Data we do NOT collect

This extension does not collect, log, transmit, or share:
— Your browsing history
— Your Suno account information
— Your audio files
— Any personally identifiable information

Local storage

The extension stores only your processing preferences (stem count, MIDI toggle) in Chrome's local storage on your device. This data never leaves your machine.

Network requests

The only network requests made by this extension are:
1. To http://127.0.0.1:5432 (your local machine) — to communicate with the Companion daemon
2. To https://*.suno.ai — to resolve audio file URLs from Suno's CDN

No requests are made to any third-party servers controlled by Smash Daddys or Raw Convict Records.
