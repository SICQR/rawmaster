RAWMASTER — A/B Blind Listening Test

Compare stem separation quality. Which sounds better — A or B? You won't know which is which until you export.

How to use:
1. Drop two folders of stems below (e.g., "demucs_only" and "rawmaster_full")
2. For each stem, listen to A and B (randomly assigned)
3. Vote which sounds better — or "Tie" if you can't tell
4. Export results to see which pipeline won
